Sticky to top Supra bar + Navbar example

This example is a quick exercise to illustrate how sticky to top supra bar + nav bar work when put together.

From 'md' breakpoint to upper, as you scroll down, the navbar will disappear and only the supra bar will remain visible at the top of your browser’s viewport. When scrolling back up, the navbar will reappear below the supra bar.
