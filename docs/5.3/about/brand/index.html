Orange brand guidelines

Documentation and examples for Orange’s logo and brand usage guidelines.

On this page

  • Orange logo
    • Responsive SVG
  • Orange Business Services logo
Boosted is based on Bootstrap, which has its own logo and brand usage guidelines.

Have a need for Boosted’s brand resources? Great! We have only a few guidelines we follow, and in turn ask you to follow as well. These guidelines were published by Orange SA on the Orange Design System website.
